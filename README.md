## Projects

### 01. FinBank Loan Portfolio Analysis
**Industry:** Financial Services

**Business Problem:** Understand portfolio health,
identify credit risk patterns, and evaluate agent
performance across five branches.

**Key Findings:**
- 80% default rate on concluded loans
- All defaults concentrated in personal loans issued
  to customers with credit scores below 550
- Nakuru branch carries an 80% default rate while
  all other branches show zero defaults
- Team A processes loans 0.5 days faster than Team B

**Skills:** CTEs, CASE WHEN, DATEDIFF, DENSE_RANK,
Multi-table JOINs, LEFT JOIN vs INNER JOIN

[View Project](./01-finbank-loan-analysis)

### 02. ShopZetu E-Commerce Analysis
**Industry:** Retail and E-Commerce

**Business Problem:** Understand revenue trends,
identify high-value customers, evaluate product
performance, and segment customers by behaviour.

**Key Findings:**
- Revenue peaked at KES 24,700 in January 2024
  before declining sharply mid-year
- Zero new customers acquired since October 2023
- 70% repeat purchase rate, nearly double the
  40% industry benchmark
- RFM segmentation: 2 Champions, 9 Loyal,
  4 At Risk, 5 Lost

**Skills:** Chained CTEs, NTILE Window Function,
LEAD and LAG, RFM Segmentation, DENSE_RANK,
Repeat Purchase Analysis
