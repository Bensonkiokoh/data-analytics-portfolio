# ShopZetu E-Commerce Analysis

## Business Context
ShopZetu is an online fashion and lifestyle 
store serving customers across Kenya. The 
analysis was commissioned to understand 
revenue trends, customer value, product 
performance, and repeat purchase behaviour.

## Dataset
- 4 tables: Customers, Orders, Order Items, 
  Products
- 52 orders across 20 customers
- 20 products across 8 categories
- Period: January 2023 to July 2024

## Business Questions Answered
1. How has monthly revenue trended?
2. How many new customers join each month?
3. Which products generate the most and 
   least revenue?
4. How is revenue distributed by category?
5. What is the repeat purchase rate?
6. How many days between first and second 
   purchase?
7. How do customers segment by RFM?

## Key Findings
- Revenue peaked at KES 24,700 in January 
  2024 before declining sharply
- Zero new customers acquired since 
  October 2023 -- concentration risk
- 70% repeat purchase rate, nearly double 
  the 40% industry benchmark
- Average 114 days between first and second 
  purchase -- re-engagement window identified
- RFM segmentation: 2 Champions, 9 Loyal, 
  4 At Risk, 5 Lost
